About ClearOut

Solving the college moving nightmare, one semester at a time.

Our Story

ClearOut was created by a college student who was tired of watching fellow peers struggle with the same problem every semester: What do you do with all your stuff when moving?

The constant cycle of expensive shipping costs, cramped car rides, and throwing away perfectly good items was a logistical nightmare that needed a solution. ClearOut transforms this chaos into a simple, sustainable system where students can donate, sell, or lease items to each other on campus.

Our Mission

To eliminate the stress of college moving by connecting students through a community-driven platform that reduces waste and saves money.
