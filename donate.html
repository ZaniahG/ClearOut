Knight's Pantry at UCF

The Knight's Pantry provides free food, clothing, and household items to UCF students in need.

Donation Hours:

  • Monday – Friday: 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM
  • Saturday: 12:00 PM – 5:00 PM
  • Sunday: Closed

Accepted Items:

  • Non-perishable food items
  • Clothing in good condition
  • Small household goods
  • School supplies

Items NOT Accepted:

  • Opened or expired food
  • Damaged clothing
  • Large furniture
  • Hazardous materials

ClearOut Temporary Donation Bins

Year-round donation bins are located at:

  • Nike Residence Hall lobby and laundry rooms.
  • Libra Residence Hall lobby and laundry rooms.

Drop off small, reusable items that you no longer need. Items are collected regularly and redistributed to students or local charities.
