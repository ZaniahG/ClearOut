Ready to ClearOut?

ClearOut is a community-based service that allows college students to donate/sell or lease items on campus for free. ClearOut makes it easy to give your things a second life by sorting, donating, or selling them as a last resort.

One Click to Barter

Sell/Donate any item (clothes, furniture) to each other on campus. Donate any uneaten food item to your local school pantry or each other!

A Easy Way

No stress on moving out of your dorm or apartment. Lease a vacumn, broom, anything else to your peers for a couple of days to a week!
